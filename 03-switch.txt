** Programa de prueba: switch (variante diferencial)
PROGRAM Switch.
DIVISION DATA.
PIC 9 dia = 3.
DIVISION PROCEDURE.
SWITCH dia.
    WHEN 1:
        DISPLAY "Lunes".
        BREAK.
    WHEN 2:
        DISPLAY "Martes".
        BREAK.
    WHEN 3:
        DISPLAY "Miercoles".
        BREAK.
    DEFAULT:
        DISPLAY "Otro dia".
END-SWITCH.
STOP RUN.
