** Programa de prueba: switch (variante diferencial)
PROGRAM Switch.
DIVISION DATA.
PIC I dia = 5.
DIVISION PROCEDURE.
EVALUATE dia
    WHEN 1.
        DISPLAY "Lunes".
        STOP CASE.
    WHEN 2.
        DISPLAY "Martes".
        STOP CASE.
    WHEN 3.
        DISPLAY "Miercoles".
        STOP CASE.
    WHEN OTHER.
        DISPLAY "Otro dia".
END-EVALUATE.
STOP RUN.
